Details for Step 1


  • Step 1-1: Locate the power button.

  • Step 1-2: Quick-press the power button. If computer screen turns on, then you are all set; otherwise, continue to Step 1.3 below.

  • Step 1-3: If power button is pressed and the computer screen does not turn on, then try again. If this does not work after several attempts, please send your computer to a repair shop.
